General Questions

What is Split Lease and what do you do?

+

Split Lease is a platform that connects people who need temporary housing with hosts who have spaces available. We specialize in flexible, short-term rentals perfect for hybrid workers, commuters, and travelers who need accommodation for specific days of the week or month.

What's the easiest way to get started?

+

Getting started is simple:

  1. Create a free account on Split Lease
  2. Browse available spaces in your desired location
  3. Book your trial night or schedule a viewing
  4. Complete your booking and enjoy your stay!

What are ways to get the best price and most available spaces?

+

To find the best deals and availability:

  • Book in advance for better pricing
  • Consider flexible dates for more options
  • Look for weekly or monthly packages
  • Set up alerts for your preferred locations
  • Be open to different neighborhoods or slightly longer commutes

What things can I store at my Split Lease rental?

+

Most rentals allow you to store personal belongings, clothing, toiletries, and work equipment. However, storage options vary by property. Always check with your host about specific storage arrangements and what's included in your rental agreement.

Who would benefit most from Split Lease?

+

Split Lease is perfect for:

  • Hybrid workers who commute to the city a few days per week
  • Business travelers with recurring trips
  • Students with internships or part-time programs
  • Digital nomads seeking flexible arrangements
  • Anyone looking for cost-effective alternatives to hotels or full-time apartments

How do I benefit (as a hybrid-work-commuter) from this service?

+

As a hybrid worker, you can:

  • Save money by only paying for the nights you need
  • Reduce commute time and stress
  • Maintain a consistent space near your office
  • Avoid expensive hotels or full-time rent
  • Have a dedicated workspace in the city

How is the Split Lease experience different from Airbnb?

+

While Airbnb focuses on vacation rentals, Split Lease specializes in recurring, flexible arrangements for workers and commuters. We offer weekly patterns, longer-term consistency, and are optimized for people who need the same space regularly rather than one-off stays.

What makes this service better than Craigslist?

+

Split Lease offers:

  • Verified hosts and secure payments
  • Professional customer support
  • Standardized booking process
  • Reviews and ratings system
  • Legal protections and guarantees
  • Easy-to-use platform with instant booking

What kinds of people would benefit most from trial nights?

+

Trial nights are ideal for:

  • First-time users wanting to test the service
  • People considering a long-term arrangement
  • Those who want to experience the location before committing
  • Anyone unsure about the host or property fit

Getting Started

How do I create an account?

+

Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a password, and complete your profile. You can also sign up using Google or Facebook for faster registration.

Do I need to verify my identity?

+

Yes, for security purposes we require identity verification. You'll need to upload a government-issued ID and take a selfie. This helps create a trusted community for both guests and hosts.

How do I search for available spaces?

+

Use our search bar to enter your desired location, dates, and any specific requirements. You can filter results by price, amenities, property type, and more to find the perfect match for your needs.

Before You Book

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

What is your cancellation policy?

+

Cancellation policies vary by host but typically include:

  • Flexible: Full refund up to 24 hours before check-in
  • Moderate: Full refund up to 5 days before check-in
  • Strict: 50% refund up to 7 days before check-in

The specific policy will be clearly displayed before you book.

Can I message the host before booking?

+

Absolutely! We encourage guests to communicate with hosts before booking. You can ask questions about the space, amenities, house rules, or anything else that will help you make an informed decision.

Are there any additional fees?

+

The total price includes the nightly rate plus a service fee (typically 10-15% of the booking subtotal). Some hosts may also charge a cleaning fee or security deposit. All fees are clearly displayed before you confirm your booking.

Listing Your Space

How do I list my property on Split Lease?

+

Click "Host with Us" in the navigation menu and follow our step-by-step process. You'll need to provide details about your space, upload photos, set your availability and pricing, and complete verification. The entire process takes about 15-20 minutes.

What photos should I include in my listing?

+

Include clear, well-lit photos of:

  • The bedroom(s) from multiple angles
  • Bathroom facilities
  • Kitchen and common areas
  • Any workspace or desk areas
  • Parking or entrance areas
  • Special amenities

High-quality photos can increase bookings by up to 40%!

How do I set my pricing?

+

Consider factors like location, amenities, competition, and demand when setting prices. Our platform provides pricing suggestions based on similar listings in your area. You can also offer weekly or monthly discounts to attract longer-term guests.

Can I block certain dates?

+

Yes! You have complete control over your calendar. You can block dates when your space isn't available, set minimum stay requirements, and adjust availability patterns for recurring bookings.

How do I handle guest check-in?

+

You can choose from several check-in methods:

  • In-person greeting
  • Lockbox with key code
  • Smart lock with unique codes
  • Building concierge or doorman

Provide clear check-in instructions to your guests before their arrival.

Legal, Taxes & Agreements

Do I need permission from my landlord to host?

+

If you rent your property, you should check your lease agreement and get written permission from your landlord before listing on Split Lease. Many leases prohibit subletting or short-term rentals.

What are my tax obligations as a host?

+

Rental income is typically taxable. You may need to:

  • Report income on your tax return
  • Pay occupancy or lodging taxes
  • Keep records of expenses for deductions

Tax requirements vary by location. We recommend consulting with a tax professional for personalized advice.

What insurance do I need?

+

Split Lease provides Host Protection Insurance up to $1 million for property damage. However, we recommend:

  • Informing your homeowners or renters insurance provider
  • Considering additional short-term rental insurance
  • Understanding what's covered and what's not

What happens if a guest damages my property?

+

If damage occurs:

  1. Document the damage with photos immediately
  2. Contact Split Lease support within 24 hours
  3. File a damage claim through our resolution center
  4. Our team will review and facilitate resolution

Our Host Protection Insurance covers eligible damages up to $1 million.
